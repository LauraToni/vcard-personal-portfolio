About me

Hi, I’m Laura, a PhD Candidate in Neuroengineering at the MINE Lab, a joint research group between Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna and Università Vita-Salute San Raffaele.

I’m a physicist by training with a strong passion for computational neuroscience, neural engineering, and medical applications.

My research focuses on developing personalized neural stimulation strategies to restore motor function in individuals with spinal cord injury, combining experimental approaches with computational modeling.
